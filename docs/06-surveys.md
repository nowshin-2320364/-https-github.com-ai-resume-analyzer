# Quantitative Survey Results

**Target Demographic:** 25 Final-Year Computer Science Students
**Methodology:** 5-Question Google Form

**Key Findings:**
1. **85%** of respondents spend more than 15 minutes tailoring their resume for a single application.
2. **92%** have experienced the "black hole" effect (applying and never hearing back).
3. **70%** do not know how ATS systems actually parse their PDF documents.
4. When asked what feature would help most, **65%** voted for "Identifying missing skills I forgot to include" and **35%** voted for "Rewriting weak bullet points."
