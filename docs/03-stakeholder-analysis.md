# Stakeholder Analysis

## Primary Stakeholders
**1. Job Seekers / Students**
- **Needs:** Fast, accurate, and actionable feedback on their resumes prior to job applications.
- **Pain Points:** Rejection without feedback; confusion over ATS formatting rules.

## Secondary Stakeholders
**1. University Career Offices**
- **Needs:** Scalable tools to assist large student bodies with resume formatting.
- **Pain Points:** Limited counselor time for 1-on-1 manual resume reviews.

**2. Academic Evaluators**
- **Needs:** Clear documentation, functioning codebases, and adherence to software engineering principles to grade the final year project.
