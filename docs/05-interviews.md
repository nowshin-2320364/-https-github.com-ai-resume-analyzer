# Qualitative Interviews

## Interviewee 1: Graduating CS Student
**Q: What is the most frustrating part of applying for jobs?**
**A:** "Tailoring my resume for every single job description takes hours. I never know if I am highlighting the right class projects, and half the time, I feel like my resume isn't even being read by a human."

## Interviewee 2: University Career Counselor
**Q: Where do students fail most often on their resumes?**
**A:** "They write their experience like a job description rather than an achievement list. They say 'Responsible for database,' instead of 'Optimized database queries to reduce load time by 20%.' They need help phrasing things with action verbs."
